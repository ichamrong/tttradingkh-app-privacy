Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how TTTrading KH ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our internal point-of-sale and inventory application (the "App").

1. Scope

This App is intended for internal use only by employees and authorized staff of TTTrading KH and is not available to the general public. Only users with accounts created by our administrators can access and use the App.

2. Information We Collect

Through the App, we may collect and process the following types of information:

  • Account and login information: username, password (stored securely), and related authentication data.
  • Business data: product details, prices, stock levels, customer information, order and transaction records, and other data entered by employees as part of their work.
  • Technical and usage data: device information, IP address, timestamps, and basic usage logs necessary to operate and secure the App.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information collected through the App to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our POS and inventory functions.
  • Manage internal sales operations, customer records, and product catalogues.
  • Ensure security, prevent unauthorized access, and detect fraud.
  • Analyze usage in order to improve our internal tools and processes.

4. Legal Basis and Internal Use

Information processed through the App is handled as part of our legitimate business interests in managing internal operations and fulfilling our obligations to customers. Access to the App and to related data is limited to authorized employees and service providers who need it for their work.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent information collected through the App to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who host, support, or maintain our IT systems, subject to appropriate confidentiality and security obligations.
  • Authorities or third parties when required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information processed through the App against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain business and account information for as long as necessary to operate our internal systems, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When information is no longer needed, we will delete or anonymize it in accordance with our retention policies.

8. Your Responsibilities as an Employee User

Employees and authorized users must keep their login credentials confidential and use the App only for legitimate business purposes in accordance with our internal policies and any applicable employment agreements.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children or by the general public. Only authorized employees and staff may use the App.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted will be deemed acceptance of those changes.
